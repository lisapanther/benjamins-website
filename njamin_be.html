Benjamin Deboosere

From August 2012 until September 2013, Benjamin Deboosere and Wouter De Raeve conducted research on the temporary state of Tem- pelhofer Feld on a self-initiated basis as well as within the framework of the artistic research programme Air Berlin Alexanderplatz. Their research aimed to address the following two questions: 'Why is Tempelhofer Feld anno 2013 such an interesting space?' and 'How can Tempelhofer Feld keep its potential?' While interviewing a variety of international protagonists on the field of architecture, sociology, urban geography and philosophy, De Raeve and Deboosere critically approached these research questions. The potential of the site was visualized through pictures taken by Deboosere, which convey the feeling present at Tempelhofer Feld during the summer of 2013. These will be collected in the book 'On Tempelhofer Feld' which will be published in the autumn of 2014.
